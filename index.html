John Dalton · 1803

Solid Sphere Model

Dalton described atoms as tiny, solid, indivisible spheres. He proposed that atoms of the same element are identical and that compounds are made from combinations of atoms.

Strength

Helped explain that each element has its own type of atom.

Limitation

Atoms are not indivisible; they contain smaller particles.

Think about it:

Why was Dalton's model useful even though it was not complete?
